# Tata Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Effective Insights

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Tata Virtual Experience Program offered through Forage.

The objective was to analyze retail sales data and create visualizations that provide actionable insights for senior leadership, including the CEO and CMO.

## Tools Used

- Tableau
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

## Business Questions Addressed

### Question 1
Monthly Revenue Trends and Seasonality Analysis

**Objective:**
Identify trends in revenue generation and highlight seasonal patterns.

### Question 2
Top Revenue-Generating Countries

**Objective:**
Determine which countries contribute the highest revenue excluding the United Kingdom.

### Question 3
Top Customers by Revenue

**Objective:**
Identify high-value customers and analyze purchasing behavior.

### Question 4
Regional Demand Analysis

**Objective:**
Visualize demand across countries and regions to support expansion strategies.

## Key Insights

- Revenue showed clear seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.
- Certain international markets contributed significantly to total revenue.
- A small group of customers generated a large proportion of overall sales.
- High-demand regions were identified for future business expansion.
